About Us

We are a voluntary, non-profit, student-run organization. Our goal is to foster cross cultural interaction transcending all geographical boundaries as well providing a strong support system that thrives to provide personal and professional assistance to the students at Montana State University in Bozeman, Montana.

Contact Us

We help incoming students with airport pickup, finding housing near campus and welcome any questions you have for us. Please do not hesitate to send us an email or use our contact form.
